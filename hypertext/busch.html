  • SHI Stadium

    SHI Stadium, located on Rutgers University's Busch Campus in Piscataway, NJ, is the 52,454-seat home of the Scarlet Knights football team and lacrosse teams. In 2009, Rutgers, the Birthplace of College Football, completed a $102 million expansion of SHI Stadium to increase the capacity to 52,454.
