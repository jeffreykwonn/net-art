Livingston Campus

Select an activity to continue your Rutgers journey.

  • Ecological Reserve

    The Ecological Reserve has hiking trails that are accessible at the South end of the campus. There are multiple entrances and the trails range anywhere from 0.5 miles to 3.5 miles long. The difficulty is easy and the trails are well maintained.

  • Rutgers Club

    The Rutgers Club is located on the second floor of Livingston Dining Commons in Piscataway. Members enjoy special culinary delights and programming in a unique collegiate and charming atmosphere.
