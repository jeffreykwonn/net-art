College Avenue Campus

Select an activity to continue your Rutgers journey.

  • College Ave Gym

    CAG is the larger of the two gyms on College Ave. It has a weightlifting room, basketball courts, a swimming pool, a bouldering section, a sauna, and more.

  • Atrium

    The Atrium is the dining hall on this campus. Students can use meal swipes or pay with cash/card at a variety of vendors that include burgers, mexican, Chinese, and salads.
